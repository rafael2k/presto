Notes:

  • Overhead for table index is not included in the sizes.
  • Multi-byte encodings have two reverse tables each, they are listed separately in the list above.
  • Entries showing a dash (—) means no table required.
  • Forward conversion table for codepage 1252 (windows-1252) is required.
  • Big 5 HKSCS encoding support in addition requires Big 5 tables.
  • ISO 2022-CN encoding support requires GBK and EUC-TW tables.
  • GBK, GB2312 and HZ-GB2312 support share data tables.
  • GB 18030 encoding support in addition requires GBK tables.
  • ISO 2022-JP, EUC-JP and Shift-JIS support share data tables.
  • EUC-JP and ISO 2022-JP-1 support for JIS-0212 can be dropped without severely affecting rendering.
  • EUC-KR and ISO 2022-KR support share data tables.
