I-Opera 11.60 isikulindile

Le nguqulo entsha ithuthukiswe ngokuzinza kanye nokusebenza, futhi senze izici ezintsha ukwenza ukuphequlula kwakho kube okuhlakaniphile futhi nokulula.

Gcina amakhasi akho ayintandokazi ngokuchofoza nje. Manje usungakwazi ukwengeza amakhasi kumaBhukhimaki noma Ukudayela Ngokushesha ngokuphazima kweso, ngokukhetha inkanyezi nje kuphela kwinkundla yekheli.

Inkundla yekheli ye-Opera nayo futhi ilungiswe ngokusikiselwayo kokucinga okusha, ukwenza kube lula ukuba uthole izingosi zakho eziyintandokazi ohlwini.

Phakathi kwezintuthuko eziningi kwinjini yesiphequluli, I-Opera 11.60 ihlanganisa isizawuli esihambelana ne-HTML5. Lokhu kunikeza ukusebenza okunothile kubathuthukisi bewebhu kanye nokuhambelana okuthuthukisiwe nabanye abaphequluli.

Ngaphezu kwalokho, ikhasimende lethu lemeyli elakhelwe ngaphakathi lenziwe lahambelana nomklamo omusha kanye nokuzulazula kokuzibonela ngokwengeziwe.
