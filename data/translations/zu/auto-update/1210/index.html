I-Opera 12.10 isifikile!

Le nguqulo entsha yesiphequluli se-Opera iletha uhlu olude lokuthuthukile ukwenza isipiloyoni sakho sokuphequlula ukuthi sisheshe futhi sibelula! Nakhu ukugqama okumbalwa:

Singeza usekelo lwezinga lenethiwekhi ye-SPDY, olunqamula izikhathi zokulayisha ikhasi. I-Gmail, Twitter.com namanye amasayithi adumile asevele asebenzisa i-SPDY.

Nge-Opera entsha, kunezandiso eziningi ongazisebenzisa ukwenza impilo ukuthi ibelula. Phequlula ngesigaba noma ubuningi ku- addons.opera.com.

I-Opera 12.10 isebenza ngcono ngamasistimu wokusebenza amaningi nezingxenyekazi. Sifake phakathi usekelo lokuthinta le-Opera ku-Windows 8 Classic naku-Windows 7, ngenkathi abasebenzisi be-Mac bazokwazi ukuthatha okuhle kwamandla amasha we-OS X Mountain Lion nge-Opera, okufaka phakathi umsebenzi wokwabelana we-Mountain Lion.

Ufuna ukwazi kabanzi? Vakashela okusha ukuthola ulwazi olubanzi kule nguqulo noma chofoza lapha ukuze ubone uhlu olugcwele lokuthuthukile komsebenzi.
