Le nguqulo entsha yesiphequluli se-Opera iletha uhlu olude lokuthuthukile ukwenza isipiloyoni sakho sokuphequlula ukuthi sisheshe futhi sibelula! Nakhu ukugqama okumbalwa:

Singeza usekelo lwezinga lenethiwekhi ye-SPDY, olunqamula izikhathi zokulayisha ikhasi. I-Gmail, Twitter.com namanye amasayithi adumile asevele asebenzisa i-SPDY.

With the new Opera, there are even more extensions you can use to make your life easier. Phequlula ngesigaba noma ubuningi ku- addons.opera.com.

I-Opera 12.10 isebenza ngcono ngamasistimu wokusebenza amaningi nezingxenyekazi.
