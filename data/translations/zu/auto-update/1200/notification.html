I-Opera 12.00 isifikile!

Lenhlobo enthsa yesiphequluli se-Opera iletha uhla olusha lwezintuthuko okuzokwenza ukuthi ukuphequlula kwakho kube ngcono, kusheshe futhi kube lula kakhulu!

Ngokuqalisa okusheshayo kanye nokulayishwa kwekhasi okusheshayo, i-Opera 12.00 ikufaka emoyeni ngokukhulu ukushesha. Kanti futhi ukuba khona kwezinhlelo ezinethebhu ephendulayo kwenza ukuthi ukwazi ukuqalisa noma ukucisha ngokuphazima nje kweso.

Yena i-Opera ibe ngeyakho ngokuthi usebenzise uhlu olusha lwezingqikithi. Shintsha indlela isiphequluli esibukeka ngayo ngokuvama okuthandwa uwena - kwenzeka nje ngokuchofoza kanye. Awudingi ngisho ukuqala kabusha!

Nge-Opera 12.00, izintandokazi zamawebusayithi akho zingasebenzisa ikhamera yewebhu yekhompyutha yakho ukwenzela ukuthi kube lula ukuthi ushuthe izithombe ngokushesha bese wabelana ngazo emoyeni.

I-Opera 12.00 iqukethe i-LTML5 yokudonsa ubeke, ukwesekwa kwe 64-bit, ukwesekwa kwezilimi zakwesokudla kuya kwesokunxele kanye nokuncipha kwamathuba okufahlaka kanye nezintuthuko eziningi enjinini yesiphequluli.
